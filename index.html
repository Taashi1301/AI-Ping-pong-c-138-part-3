AI Ping Pong











Developed by Ronak Prakash

Instructions

1. Keep your laptop screen straight. 2. Stay 3-4 feet distanced from the laptop's webcam, facing it. 3. Move you right wrist up and down, you would be able to see a red dot appear on it. 4. Press the play button, and go to the same spot you were before. 5. The red paddle is you paddle, which will move according to your right wrist's movement.
